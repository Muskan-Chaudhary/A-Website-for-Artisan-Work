CERAMICS

HAVE A MEAL WITH THEM

A collection of one-off pieces, this set uses raw textures and naked clay to bring out the earthiness of the earthenware. The series is named ‘Back to Earth’ and aspires to create an image of how things would have ‘formed’ without the use of machines. Despite being handmade, I have tried to keep the pieces lightweight and sleek.
