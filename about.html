The Makers, Designers, Engineers

Engineering students, MNC is a design enthusiast team whose work revolves around exploring design solutions rooted in context and appropriate for the local region. Their ceramic work is at the intersection of engineering and art – aspiring to create the function without being ‘machinic’. They all types of hand building techniques (pinch pots, coils, and slabs) in their work. In fact, these were the techniques used by potters before they got the wheel. They believe that not using the wheel let them express themselves without any limits. Inspired by nature, they do not believe in creating perfectly symmetric pieces.
